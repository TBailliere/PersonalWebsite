About Me

I was born in Belgium but moved to Sweden when I was 8 years old. In Sweden, I attended international schools, where the subject-matter was all taught in English. Hence, I feel as though English is my native language, rather than Dutch. After graduating high school in Sweden, I taught English in China for a while before coming back to Europe for my studies.

Fast forward a few years and I found myself at a crossroads. I wasn’t enjoying the job I held and wanted more out of my life. I dread being a cog in the machine. I want to create. After much searching, deliberating and consulting with others, I decided to go back to school and enroll at Thomas More hogeschool (school for advanced education) to obtain a graduaat (associate's degree) in programming. In case you are interested in what exactly I will be learning throughout this program, I encourage you to press the button below to find out more. I’m excited to share this journey with you!
